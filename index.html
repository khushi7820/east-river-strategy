Client Impact

From inherited playbook to a defensible position.

The Problem

Scale without a story.

The leadership team had built a sprawling portfolio of clinics but no unifying thesis. Every regional director was running a different playbook, and patients could feel the inconsistency.

Our Approach

Start with the patient, not the org chart.

We ran twelve weeks of field research with patients, physicians, and frontline staff. We rebuilt the strategy from the ground up around three differentiated segments — and restructured the leadership team to match.

The Outcome

A clearer bet. A more confident team.

Within a year, Vantage had a strategy its leaders could articulate in a sentence, two new growth platforms in market, and an executive team aligned on what the next four years require.

Our Philosophy

A consulting firm built for the questions that don't fit the slide.

East River Strategy was founded on a conviction: the hardest problems in business aren't solved by more data or better frameworks. They're solved by leaders willing to slow down, ask sharper questions, and commit to answers they can live with.

We work in small, senior teams. We stay close to the work. We don't parachute in with prefabricated methodologies, and we don't leave organizations more dependent on us than when we arrived. Our measure of success is simple: are our clients making better decisions — and holding themselves to a higher standard — a year after we're gone.
